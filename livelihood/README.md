# Livelihood Domain

Schema specification for the **Livelihood** domain in OPuN networks. This domain covers job discovery, applications, interviews, offers, and employment fulfillment on Beckn Protocol v2.0.

## Scope

- **Job discovery and catalog** — Search, filtering, and catalog publication of job opportunities
- **Application lifecycle** — Application forms, submission, status tracking, document management
- **Interviews and assessments** — Interview scheduling, stages, types, and modes
- **Offers and employment** — Job offers, acceptance, joining, and fulfillment tracking
